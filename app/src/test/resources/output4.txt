Player A: you are going to place the following ships. For each ship,
type the coordinate of the upper left side of the ship, followed by orientation:
H (for horizontal), V (fo rvertical), U (for up), R (for right), D (for down), L (for left)
For example M4H would place a ship horizontally starting at M4 and going to the right.
You have

2 "Submarines" ships that are 1x2 (H or V)
3 "Destroyers" that are 1x3 (H or V)
3 "Battleships" that are: 

 *b              B         Bbb        *b
 bbb    OR       bb   OR    b     OR  bb 
                 b                     b 

  Up             Right      Down      Left

2 "Carriers" that are:

 C                       C             
 c          *cccc        cc       * ccc
 cc   OR    ccc      OR  cc   OR  cccc 
 cc                       c         
  c                       c

 Up         Right       Down      Left
